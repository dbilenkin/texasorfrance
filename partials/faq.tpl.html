What's the deal with the scoring?

There is a difficulty and a streak multiplier.
The difficulty multiplier is based on the ratio of the areas and the overall sizes of the countries. Larger countries are easier than smaller ones. 

this.difficultyMultiplier = Math.ceil((20 - Math.log(largerArea))/Math.sqrt(ratio))
The streak multiplier increases with every answer you get right and decreases with every answer you get wrong. 
var streakMultiplier = Math.floor(this.streakMultiplierStart * Math.pow(1.2,this.streak));
